About Me

I am an aspiring developer currently living in Brunswick, Ohio. In 2018, I began creating games on Roblox after watching my son using the company's game engine. When I started that journey, I figured I would learn a few cool things to show my children and bond with them threw learning together. However, using code to make a project come to life has become a passion of mine. I believe there is nothing as euphoric as the moment when I see something I created work as I intended.

I am committed to gaining a well-rounded education and becoming the best developer possible. Having no traditional training in computer science, I have enrolled in the Google IT Support Professional Certificate Program on Coursera and will seek the CompTIA A+ certification. I believe my eagerness to learn, previous experience with Lua, experience from previous careers, and passion for creating with code make me an excellent candidate for the position of Web Development Apprentice with thunder::tech.
